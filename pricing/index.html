Flexible pricing, to meet your needs.

FLOs are streams of programming logic that are the basis for your application.

You can use many FLOs to drive complex functionality, or a single FLO for a specific purpose.
Pay for only what you use.

Each FLO costs $0.14 per hour it is powered on. Inside Azuqua, you can toggle the status of a FLO to be active, or inactive, whenever you'd like. While a FLO is toggled to active, it is charged at the aforementioned price.
