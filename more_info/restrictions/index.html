What is Azuqua?

Low code development.

You develop your apps inside our visual environment via an intuitive (and beautiful) drag-and-drop interface.

Azuqua is an integration-first platform, meaning that we look at the world in terms of connecting things together.

Powerful, easy, and fast.

"Low code" is often synonymous with "restrictive". This doesn't have to be true. We believe that powerful and easy don't have to be mutually exclusive. Azuqua most closely resembles a visual programming language, but without the ick factor.

You'll find all your old friends, such as arrays, strings, objects, booleans, and numbers, they just might be named funny.

Microservices, everywhere.

The most successful applications no longer work in isolation. Instead, they are a web of interconnected services. RESTful architecture and a healthy, robust application ecosystem have started replacing the monolithic structures of the past.

Apps built on Azuqua naturally support this development paradigm.

The API economy.

More and more services are making their APIs available to the public. They recognize the mutual value to be gained by allowing developers to leverage their existing services.

Leveraging the API economy means recognizing the value in connecting the dots.

Don't spend your time reinventing the wheel. Spend your time creating an entirely new car.
