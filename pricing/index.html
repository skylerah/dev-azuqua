Pricing

FLOs are streams of application logic that are the foundation of your application.

Pay only for what you use.

Each FLO costs $0.14 per hour of utilization. Inside Azuqua, you can toggle the status of a FLO to be active, or inactive, whenever you'd like.

Join our beta Developer Program.

We're offering early access to developers while our Developer Program is in beta. This plan will allow you to build, explore, and create on the Azuqua Platform, at no cost.

This early access period is limited to non-commercial applications (although we are very happy to discuss what it would take to allow a commercial solution!).

Once the early access period is over, our pricing will be changed to our standard $0.14 per hour of FLO utilization. Don't worry, you will be able to keep all existing FLOs you have created during this period of time!
